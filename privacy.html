Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2025

Introduction

Meeting Optimizer ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Our extension only accesses the following information:

  • Calendar Data: We request read-only access to your Google Calendar events to identify upcoming meetings with video conference links.
  • Browser Tabs: We create and manage browser tabs for your meeting links.

We do not collect personal information beyond what is necessary for the functioning of the extension.

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Detect meetings in your calendar with video conference links
  • Open meeting links in browser tabs before your scheduled meetings
  • Manage these tabs according to your preferences

All processing occurs locally in your browser. We do not store or transmit your calendar data to external servers.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share or sell your data with third parties. Your calendar information never leaves your browser.

Data Security

The extension operates entirely within your browser and communicates directly with Google's APIs using secure methods.

Your Rights

You can control the extension's access to your data at any time by:

  • Disabling or removing the extension from Chrome
  • Revoking calendar access permissions from your Google Account settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
